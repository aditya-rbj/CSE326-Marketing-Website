About Us

In high school, I was that kid that seemed to never be paying attention. Seemingly in my own world of doodles and drawings. I have had a pencil in my hand since i was in diapers. Things never change..I am no longer in diapers though. When i started college i originally had the idea of going into animation. After building my first website on Geocities, and getting instant gratification of praise from others, I was hooked. The internet was my outlet. A new way to show and share my passions. I do the work i do because i love it. I like the industry. I like the people i meet. I like to talk shop. I embrace the nerd. I like to make the web a better place. Hopefully we cross paths in the real world someday and we can make it a better place together.
